PalVoice

PalVoice is developed by a highly dedicated professional team committed to supporting the struggle of the Palestinian people. We are composed of technology students and humanitarian activists who believe that technology can be a powerful tool for creating change.

With diverse backgrounds, we combine our expertise to build this platform with the aim of facilitating communication, fundraising, and disseminating accurate and in-depth information. We are determined to continue innovating and expanding the reach of PalVoice to support a larger and sustainable struggle for the Palestinian people.
